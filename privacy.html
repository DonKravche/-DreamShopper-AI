Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

DreamShopper AI does not store your personal browsing history. When you use the "Analyze" feature, the extension temporarily reads the product title and price of the current page to provide AI insights.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the Google Gemini API to analyze product data. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to Google. We also use Amazon Associate links; if you click a deal, Amazon may use cookies to track the referral.

3. Security

Your Gemini API key is stored locally on your device via chrome.storage and is never transmitted to our servers.
